Biography

I am Tzu-Hsuan Dennis Tseng, a master’s student in journalism interested in business analytics, statistics, data visualization, and data journalism. I currently study at National Taiwan University in Taipei, Taiwan. I like to solve problems and tell stories using data. I believe that data can help us change the world in a positive way.


Interests:

  • Statistics
  • Business Analytics
  • Data Visualization
  • Data Journalism

Education:

  • MA in Journalism (Expected), 2022
  • BBA in Business Administration, Operations Management & Business Analytics Module, 2019
